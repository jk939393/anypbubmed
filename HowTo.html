How to use PubMed Research for ChatGPT

Written by Taylor

Updated on 10/8/2023

  1. Sign Up for ChatGPT Plus: Ensure you have a ChatGPT Plus account. If not, sign up here.
  2. Enable Plugins: After signing up, enable plugins from the settings button on ChatGPT.
  3. Enable Plugins: Select The plugins menu from dropdown.
  4. Install AnyPubMed Plugin: In the Plugin store, search for "AnyPubMed" and press "Install".
  5. Sign Up for AnyPubMed: The plugin will require you to sign up and log in. Choose your preferred method.
  6. Usage: Once enabled, you can ask questions related to medical research, and the plugin will fetch relevant papers and abstracts from PubMed.
